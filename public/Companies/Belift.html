Belift Lab/BE:LIFT, previously known as BELIF+ a south korean company which is a joint project of CJ ENM and HYBE. It was founded in 2018. So far, they have done 2 survival shows wherein trainees train to become a part of a group. Those survival shows are: I-LAND - 2020 (wherein Enhypen was formed), R u Next? - 2023 (wherein ILLIT was formed.)
