Hybe is an entertainment company that started off as BigHit Entertainment founded on February 1, 2005 by Bang Si-Hyuk the first ever "group" in BigHit was called 8eight which is a vocal trio that debuted in 2007. Over the years they would sign contracts with companies like being a major shareholder of Pledis Entertainment, and after a few years making them rebrand into HYBE in March 2021 as being an entertainment lifestyle platform company. The labels under HYBE are BigHit, Pledis, ADOR, Source Music, KOZ entertainment, Hybe Labels Japan and BeLift.
