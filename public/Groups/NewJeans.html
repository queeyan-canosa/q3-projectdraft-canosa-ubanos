New Jeans is a girl group under ADOR with 5 members. Namely, Hanni, Minji, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein. Their debut date was July 22, 2022 and had their first album released on August 1, 2022 with the album, New Jeans so often called as their first EP.

Their fans' name is called "bunnies" and their official greeting is "Hello! We are New jeans!". New Jeans means a sort of word rhymes with New Jeans, which is New Gene stating that their music will be suitable for the new generation.

ADOR which stands for "All Doors One Room" is under the parent company, HYBE and was founded on November 12, 2021, with Min Hee-Jin as the CEO and a former Cheif Brand Officer in HYBE. They are considered to be an independent company. They were looking for auditionees for a New Girl group that they wanted to debut in 2022, they were selected from the Plus Global Audition during 2019. The group that came to be the result of this Global Audition was called New Jeans. They lasted in the company for two years, 2022-2024 although still pursuing their career whilst not having a contract in ADOR anymore.
